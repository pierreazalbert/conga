Optional feature: digital board

Remotely call-in your customers using a spare device (anything with an internet browser will do: phone, ipad, computer...)

  • On your spare device

    • Find your Queue Display link in your welcome email or in your admin page

    • Click on the Queue Display link of your shop and display your device it at the door

  • On your main device

    • Go to your admin panel to monitor your queue in real time

    • You can use the control buttons to either wait for the next booking, or call the next customer in line
